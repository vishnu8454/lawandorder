Roles We Hire For

The positions below represent the types of roles we regularly recruit for — submit your profile through the form below and we will reach out when a relevant opportunity arises.

Junior Advocate — Civil & Criminal Litigation
Full Time New Delhi 0 – 3 Years PQE Litigation
↓

We are seeking a motivated junior advocate to assist in day-to-day litigation before the Delhi High Court and district courts. You will draft pleadings, conduct legal research, assist in court appearances, and gradually build your own advocacy skills under senior supervision.

Requirements
  • LLB or LLM from a recognised university
  • Enrollment with the Bar Council (or in process)
  • Strong legal research and drafting skills
  • Ability to work independently and under reasonable pressure
  • Fluency in English; working knowledge of additional language, Foreign language shall be given priority
Express Interest
Corporate & IPR Associate
Full Time New Delhi 1 – 4 Years PQE Corporate
↓

We are expanding our corporate advisory and intellectual property practice and need an associate with genuine interest in transactional law, trademark and copyright matters, startup advisory, and contract drafting.

Requirements
  • LLB / LLM with a focus in corporate or commercial law
  • Experience in contract drafting and due diligence
  • Understanding of IPR laws in India (Trade Marks Act, Copyright Act)
  • Excellent communication and client-facing skills
  • Prior law-firm or in-house experience preferred
Express Interest
Senior Advocate — Writ & Constitutional Practice
Retainer / Consulting New Delhi 5 + Years PQE Litigation
↓

We welcome applications from experienced advocates interested in a retainer arrangement for constitutional and writ matters, including PIL work before the Delhi High Court and the Supreme Court of India. This is a collaborative, non-exclusive arrangement.

Requirements
  • Minimum 5 years of practice with writ / constitutional experience
  • Track record of High Court and/or Supreme Court appearances
  • Interest in access-to-justice and public interest matters
  • Strong written and oral advocacy skills
Express Interest
Legal Research & Drafting Associate
Full Time New Delhi / Hybrid Fresher / 1 Year PQE Research
↓

This role is ideal for a sharp, diligent law graduate who excels at legal research, case analysis, and written work. You will support all practice groups with research memos, brief drafting, and case preparation, building deep domain expertise across areas.

Requirements
  • LLB from a recognised Indian law school (NLU / DU / equivalent)
  • Excellent legal research skills — SCC Online, Manupatra, Westlaw
  • Strong English writing with attention to citation and structure
  • Meticulous, deadline-oriented, and proactive attitude

Internships

For Law Students

We run structured internship programmes throughout the year for law students at every stage of their studies. Applications are open on a rolling basis.

§
Litigation Internship
4 – 8 Weeks · Court-Side
Shadow advocates in court, assist in file preparation, observe hearings across civil, criminal, and writ matters. Ideal for students who want real courtroom exposure early in their education.
⚖
Research & Drafting Internship
4 – 6 Weeks · Office-Based
Work on legal memos, research assignments, and draft pleadings under supervision. Perfect for students who want to build precision in legal writing and analytical thinking.
🏛
Summer Intensive
6 – 10 Weeks · Full Immersion
A comprehensive programme combining court appearances, research, client meetings, and a supervised capstone project. Certificates provided. Best for students between 3rd and 5th year.
How to Apply

Our Process

01
Submit Application
Fill out the form below with your CV, cover letter, and any writing samples. Internship applicants may submit a brief statement of purpose.
02
Initial Review
Our team reviews all applications within 7 working days. Shortlisted candidates are contacted for an introductory conversation.
03
Interview & Assessment
A two-round process: a brief written legal research task followed by a conversation with senior advocates. We assess ability, attitude, and alignment.
04
Offer & Onboarding
Successful candidates receive a formal offer and are welcomed into the team with a structured onboarding week and mentorship assignment.
